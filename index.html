About

LiveSearchbench is a simple benchmark-style site to present real‑time search evaluation results. It aims to answer questions like:

  • How effective are systems at retrieving fresh, relevant results?
  • How do latency and quality trade off under live conditions?
  • How do different stacks compare under identical tasks?
